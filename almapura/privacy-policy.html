Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

We at AlmaPura are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and the user's rights regarding the collected data.

2. Data Collection

The AlmaPura app does not collect any personal data directly from the user. However, certain data is collected to support subscriptions and improve the app experience:

  • The Apple App Store and Google Play Store may collect data as described in their respective policies.
  • Our backend service collects user and device IDs (which are not linked to the user's true identity) and purchase history.
  • We collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how the app is used and to improve functionality. This data is not linked to individual users and can be disabled in the app settings at any time.
  • Users may optionally send anonymous feedback through the app. Feedback is not linked to user identity.

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Managing subscriptions and ensuring app functionality.
  • Improving the app by analyzing aggregated, anonymous usage patterns (if usage data collection is enabled).
  • Incorporating user-submitted anonymous feedback to enhance features and user experience.

We may share data with trusted third parties that assist us in providing the subscription service and analytics. No personal data is sold to third parties.

4. Data Deletion Requests

To request the deletion of your previously collected data, please contact us at almapura@gandg.art.

5. Local Data Storage

All other data, which is not mentioned under the "Data Collection" section, is stored locally on the user's device. If the user enable backup services provided by third parties (such as iCloud or Google Drive), the information may be stored according to the privacy policies of those services. We do not provide any backup service nor have access to the data stored there.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at almapura@gandg.art.
